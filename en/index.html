Principle of the game [source]

Rock-paper-scissors is a game performed with the hands and opposing one or more players.
In general, the stone beats the scissors (by blunting them), the scissors beat the leaf (by cutting it), the leaf beats the stone (by wrapping it).

How to play

Here the player opposes the computer for 5 rounds, in the end if the player gets a score higher than that of the computer he wins, if the score is lower then he loses.
In order to play you have to make a choice by clicking on one of the three buttons.
At the end of the 5 rounds, if the player wants to redo a game just click on Play again
